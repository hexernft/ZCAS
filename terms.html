🧁 Website Use

This website is provided to help customers view ZCAS TastyBites products, build order requests, read information, and contact ZCAS through WhatsApp.

💬 WhatsApp Orders

Orders are mainly confirmed through WhatsApp. Submitting or generating an order summary on the website does not automatically mean the order is accepted. ZCAS will confirm availability, pricing, delivery, and payment details through WhatsApp.

🧾 Order Summaries

Order summaries and PNG receipts generated on the website are for customer convenience. Final confirmation must still be done directly with ZCAS TastyBites.

🎂 Custom Cakes and Special Requests

Custom cakes, birthday cakes, wedding cakes, catering, corporate orders, and seasonal requests may require extra confirmation. Final price depends on size, flavour, design, decoration, quantity, date, and delivery requirements.

💳 Payment

Payment instructions are provided by ZCAS after an order is confirmed. Customers should only make payment after confirming the correct order details and payment method directly with ZCAS.

🚚 Delivery and Pickup

Delivery availability, cost, and timing depend on location, order size, and schedule. Pickup or delivery details should be confirmed through WhatsApp before finalizing an order.

⚠️ Allergies and Dietary Needs

Customers are responsible for informing ZCAS about allergies or dietary restrictions before placing an order. ZCAS will confirm what is possible based on the request.

📸 Product Appearance

Product images, previews, emojis, illustrations, and cake builders on the website are visual guides. Actual products may vary slightly depending on ingredients, design, size, and availability.

🔄 Order Changes

Order changes should be requested as early as possible. Changes may not always be possible after preparation has started, especially for custom cakes, bulk orders, or event orders.

📌 Updates to These Terms

ZCAS TastyBites may update these terms as the website, ordering process, and business services improve.

📞 Contact

For order questions, terms questions, or clarification before ordering, contact ZCAS TastyBites on WhatsApp: 08148124675.

Need Clarification Before Ordering?
