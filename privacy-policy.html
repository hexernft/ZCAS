Information We Collect

When you place an order, make an enquiry, or use the order builder, ZCAS TastyBites may receive information you choose to provide, such as:

  • Your name
  • Your phone number or WhatsApp contact
  • Delivery area or event location
  • Order details, cake details, catering details, or custom request notes
  • Preferred date, delivery time, or event information

How We Use Your Information

We use customer information mainly to process orders, confirm availability, prepare requests, arrange delivery or pickup, respond to questions, and improve customer service.

WhatsApp Orders

ZCAS TastyBites uses WhatsApp as the main ordering channel. When you click a WhatsApp button, your order summary may be opened inside WhatsApp so you can send it directly to ZCAS. WhatsApp use is also subject to WhatsApps own privacy terms.

Order Summary Images

Some pages may help you generate a PNG order summary. The image is created in your browser for easy sharing or download. You choose whether to download or share it.

Sharing of Information

ZCAS TastyBites does not sell customer information. Information may only be used for order handling, customer communication, delivery coordination, or where required for business operations.

Data Security

We aim to handle customer information responsibly. Please avoid sending highly sensitive information through forms or WhatsApp unless it is needed for your order.

Allergies and Special Requests

Allergy or dietary information shared with ZCAS is used only to understand your order request. Customers should always confirm allergy concerns before placing an order.

Updates to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated as the ZCAS website and ordering process improves. Updated versions can be posted on this page.

Contact ZCAS
